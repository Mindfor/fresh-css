Mobile helpers

You can specify custom behavior for the width of the blocks on mobile devices. First specify .blocks-md-5 and .blocks-md-3 for proper behavior on width of tablet and higher. Next specify .blocks-sm-3 and .blocks-sm-2 and then blocks on mobile devices will be the width of 33% or 50%, regardless of the original blocks width.
